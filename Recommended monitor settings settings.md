Recommended exhibition settings

For a 48-inch Full HD monitor:

Resolution: 1920 × 1080
Windows scaling: 100% or 125%
Display mode: Extend or Second screen only
App: Maximized

Your Live Tracking page should then show:

Large RGB view | Large depth heat map
Compact controls below

The video images will scale automatically while maintaining their aspect ratio. The scroll area will still allow access to the lower controls if Windows scaling makes the usable space smaller.

Avoid duplicate-display mode

When Windows is set to Duplicate these displays, it may use the lower resolution supported by both your laptop and the exhibition monitor.

Prefer:

Windows + P
→ Extend

Then move BioMotion Studio to the large monitor and maximize it.

Or use:

Windows + P
→ Second screen only

This is often the cleanest exhibition setup.

For a 4K monitor

A 4K television may use:

3840 × 2160

Windows might automatically apply 150% or 200% scaling. The app will still resize, but buttons and controls could become larger than expected.

Recommended starting point:

Resolution: 3840 × 2160
Scaling: 125% or 150%

For the largest possible camera views, use:

Scaling: 100% or 125%
